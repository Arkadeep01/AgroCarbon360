Privacy Policy

At AgroCarbon360, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, etc.).
  • Farming details (farm size, crop type, cooperative information).
  • Usage data such as login activity and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide access to our platform and its services.
  • To connect farmers, cooperatives, auditors, and administrators.
  • For carbon credit tracking and reporting.
  • To improve user experience and add new features.

3. Data Security

We implement strict measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no system can be 100% secure, and users are encouraged to protect their account credentials.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with authorized partners such as cooperatives, auditors, or government authorities for program verification and reporting purposes.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent to data processing.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@agrocarbon360.com

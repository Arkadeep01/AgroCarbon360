📖 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a new report?

Go to the Reports page and click "➕ Submit New Report". Fill in details and save.

Can I edit a submitted report?

Yes, go to "My Reports", select your report, and click ✏️ Edit.

What is "Carbon Saved"?

It refers to the estimated CO₂ reduction achieved through your sustainable practices.
